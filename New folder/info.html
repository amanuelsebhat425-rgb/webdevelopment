Basic Information Location: Gangodawila, Nugegoda, near Colombo, Sri Lanka Type: Public research university Established: 1959 (with origins dating back to 1873) Student population: Over 12,000 undergraduates, with additional postgraduate students It is one of the largest and most established universities in Sri Lanka Faculties and Programs The university includes a wide range of faculties: Applied Sciences Engineering Management Studies and Commerce Medical Sciences Humanities and Social Sciences Computing Technology Allied Health Sciences Dental Sciences Urban and Aquatic Bioresources Graduate Studies It is particularly well known for programs in business, finance, accounting, information technology, and science-related fields. Research and International Links The university has more than 20 research centers and focuses on innovation and academic research. It collaborates with international universities and organizations, offering exchange programs and joint research opportunities.
